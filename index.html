Technology with taste.

Design Mainline is a luxury technology studio based in Southern California. Founded by Jason Hamilton, we sit at the intersection of smart environment design, full-stack software engineering, and digital product development.

We design intelligent spaces — specifying and programming smart home automation, ambient lighting systems, home theaters, and AV installations. For the build, we partner with licensed professionals to ensure every installation meets code and exceeds expectations.

On the software side, we build production-grade applications from zero to one. Our flagship product, SkateHubba™, is a location-based social platform for skateboarders — currently in active development with patent-pending game mechanics.

Every project gets the same standard: precision engineering, luxury execution, zero shortcuts.
